What You Need

Before you can start the cooking part, you're gonna need some tools. I've found that two of the best places to find affordable cooking utensils are Walmart and Amazon. To get started with most recipes on this website, you will need the following:

  • Deep Skillet
  • Saucepan
  • Rubber Spatula
  • Cookie Sheet
  • Medium Bowl

Most of these items can be bought in a set, and if you wish to build up your collection, feel free to experiment with different sizes and materials.
