Spaghetti and Meat Sauce

Ingredients

  • ½ lb ground beef
  • ½ lb ground italian sausage
  • 28 oz tomato sauce
  • 14 oz diced tomatoes
  • tsp garlic salt
  • 1 tsp dried italian seasoning
  • 1 lb spaghetti

Instructions:

  1. Cook noodles according to package directions
  2. Brown ground beef and Italian sausage in a deep skillet until fully cooked.
  3. Add garlic salt and italian seasoning, cook for 1 minute
  4. Add tomato sauce and tomatoes
  5. Let simmer 15 minutes
  6. Drain pasta
  7. Serve as desired
